• We have multiple method to access our elements:-

1. document.getElementById("id_name");              Selecting with id.
2. document.getElementsByClassName("class_name");   Selecting with class.
3. document.getElementsByTagName("p");              Selecting with tagName (paragraph tag).

• There is also a better way or we can say a level up way to access our element which is called Query Selector.
In Query Selector we can pass our ID, Class, & TagName.

1. document.querySelector("id_name / class_name / tagName");
2. document.querySelectorAll("id_name / class_name / tagName");
